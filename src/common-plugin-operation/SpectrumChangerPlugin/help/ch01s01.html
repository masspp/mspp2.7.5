Navigating to the Next or Previous Spectrum

The next or previous spectrum can be displayed.

  1. While a spectrum is displayed in Single display mode, select [View] in the menu bar and then select [Spectrum] > [Next] or [Previous] from the drop down menu.

    The next or previous spectrum appears.

    • The next or previous spectrum can also be displayed by clicking the icon or icon in the toolbar.
