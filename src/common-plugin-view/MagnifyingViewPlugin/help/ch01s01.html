Magnifying the Selected Range of a Spectrum

The selected range of a spectrum can be magnified along the Y-axis.

  1. While a spectrum is displayed in Single display mode, right-click at one end of the range on a spectrum and drag to the other end.

    Note:

    • The mouse pointer appears as .

    • Click in the plot area as shown below and drag.

    The context menu appears.

  2. Select [Magnify].

    The Magnify dialog appears.

  3. Edit the values and click [OK].

Changing and Resetting the Magnification

  1. Double-click the right mouse button on a yellow area.

    The Magnify dialog appears.

  2. Enter a value for [Scale] and click [OK] to change the scale.

    • Click [Clear] to reset the magnification.
